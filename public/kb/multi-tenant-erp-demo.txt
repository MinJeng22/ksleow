Multi-Tenant ERP CRM Prototype
Interactive browser-based ERP prototype with login, tenant switching, CRM customer CRUD, soft deletion, audit log, B2B network links, and Malaysia LHDN e-Invoice profile checks.
URL: https://ksleow.com.my/demo/multi-tenant-erp/

schemaEntities: AuditableEntity base with soft deletion and optimistic concurrency; User and UserExternalLogin for email plus Google and Microsoft SSO; Company as tenant workspace with LHDN tax profile; CompanyUser pivot for Admin, Standard, and ReadOnly roles; Customer CRM entity scoped by CompanyId; B2BNetworkLink for cross-tenant handshake
Dashboard shell
The prototype opens into a working multi-tenant dashboard after sign-in, with an active workspace company selector, tenant-specific tax profile, RBAC role, and responsive navigation.
CRM customer list
The CRM module supports searchable customer records, add/edit forms, soft deletion, restore actions, Registered Name, BRN, TIN, Phone, Status, and responsive scaling from desktop data table to mobile cards.
Authentication
The authentication panel supports simulated Google SSO, Microsoft SSO, and company email registration for a frontend-only ERP workflow.
Operational modules
The prototype includes customer LHDN readiness checks, B2B network link accept/reject actions, and an audit log persisted in browser localStorage.
